About EthanoDrop


Keeping it going since 2020

Founded on the simple principle that no person should have to leave their house to get a drink, EthanoDrop was established in early 2020 by a couple of students. Since then other delivery companies have tried, and failed to mimic their success. The idea is and always has been to deliver "supplies" to parties and gatherings so that others don't have to. Pictured to the left you will see just some of our very qualified deliverypeople, skilled not only in the art of fast deliveries but also in the art of charm. So please join our rapidly growing community and make your life easier in the process.

How it works

The journey to an easier life begins here!

The process is quite simple, once you have created a valid account on our website and we have verified that you are of legal age,
you can start browsing our selection of products and then order them straight to your door form any device with an internet connection.

Once your order has been placed, one of our trusted couriers will pick up your order and proceed to your location posthaste.
Upon arrival the courier will once again check your ID's as an added safety precaution. In larger cities orders can be delivered using a bicycle
courier but in more remote areas they might have to be carried out using a car or some other mode of transportation

You can always track your order using our website or our mobile application, so that you can stay up to date on when the courier
will approximately arrive.
